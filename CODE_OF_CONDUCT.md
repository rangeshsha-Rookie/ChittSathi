# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and safe environment for everyone involved in ChittSaathi.

Participants of all backgrounds, identities, and experience levels are welcome.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in communication.
- Assume good intent and provide helpful feedback.
- Focus discussions on improving the project.
- Respect privacy, especially for mental-health-related topics.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing technical or community discussions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact maintainers privately:

- Email: `maintainers@chittsaathi.local` (replace with real address)

Please include details, links, and context so maintainers can investigate.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take actions including:

- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban

Enforcement decisions are made to protect community safety.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, issue and PR discussions, and related communications.
