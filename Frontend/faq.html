What is MindSpace?

MindSpace is a student-first wellness platform designed to bridge the gap between clinical mental health support and everyday wellbeing practices. It combines practical tools, guided resources, and supportive services in one place.

Our goal is to make trusted mental health support easier to access, easier to understand, and easier to use consistently.

Is my data confidential?

Yes. We follow strict privacy and security standards, including encrypted communication and secure storage practices. Your sensitive data is protected and handled responsibly.

We do not sell personal data, and access is limited to authorized workflows required to deliver support services.

How do I book an appointment?

Open the appointment section from your dashboard, choose your preferred counselor or support session, and select an available time slot.

After confirmation, you receive booking details and reminders to help you stay on track.

How does AI support work?

Our AI support provides immediate, empathetic guidance through conversation, grounding suggestions, and evidence-based self-help recommendations.

It complements professional care by helping you regulate stress between sessions and guiding you toward the right next step.

Do I need to pay to use MindSpace?

Many core features are available at no cost for students. Depending on your institution or selected services, additional premium support options may be available.

Can I reschedule or cancel an appointment?

Yes. You can manage your bookings from the appointment dashboard. We recommend making changes as early as possible so your slot can be reallocated to another student in need.
