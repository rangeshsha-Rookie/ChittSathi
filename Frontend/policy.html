Our commitment to your privacy

At ChittSaathi, we believe mental wellbeing depends on trust. Privacy is not only a legal expectation, it is a care standard we hold ourselves accountable to every day.

This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information while you use our platform and support services.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

Basic profile information such as name, email, and institution details to create and manage your account securely.

Health Information

Mood check-ins, journal activity, and wellness progress used to personalize support experiences responsibly.

Academic Context

Academic schedule and campus context to surface relevant wellbeing resources during high-stress periods.

How We Use Your Information

1

Delivering Services

We use your data to provide core platform capabilities including personalized resources, journaling tools, and support navigation.

2

Improving Experience

Aggregated and anonymized usage patterns help us improve accessibility, clarity, and effectiveness of student support journeys.

Data Security

We apply modern encryption and security controls across data storage and data transfer. Sensitive information is protected in transit and at rest using industry best practices.
